Welcome To The Office Of Research & Development (R&D)
The Office of R&D provides specialized administrative and managerial support for the operation of Sponsored Research Projects, Consultancy Projects and other R&D related activities of the Institute. The Office of R&D facilitates interaction with national and international agencies supporting research & development. Also, it promotes academia-industry interactions and collaboration with larger R&D laboratories, and liaises between Institute and funding agencies. Further, it supports Faculty Members in filing of patents and documenting IPR generated through research activity of the Institute. The Office of R&D facilitates the following:
(a) Management of Projects
Registration of Sponsored, Consulting and Institute Projects, raising Invoice/Grants-in-aid bill, receiving funds, prepare utilization certificates;
Appointment, extension and resignations of Project Staff Members; payment of salary;
Work related to Fellowships;
Project related purchases, imports and their payments;
Management of temporary advances to PIs and Project Staff Members, settlement of advances;
Payment for work assignments; and
Preparation of cheques, bank reconciliation, accounts preparation, management of investments.
(b)	Management of Office
Preparation and publishing of booklets (hard and soft copies) related to Office of R&D, newsletter;
Institute’s research magazine;
Developing & maintaining database of R&D contacts in Industry, Academia and R&D Organisations in India and abroad;
Designing and preparing R&D flyers of different Departments and laboratories of the Institute; and
Managing MOUs, IPRs, Corporate Communications, Events and Relationships.

Welcome To The Central Research Facility (CRF)
The state-of-the-art central instrumentation facility of IIT Jodhpur which was established in the year 2018 has been designated as the Central Research Facility (CRF). The CRF has an endeavour to provide advanced scientific instrumentation service in the multidisciplinary field of research to the undergraduate, graduate, Ph.D students and the faculties of this esteemed institute as well as researcher from other institutes across the country. At present a total number of 93 high end instruments are under CRF facility. Currently, The space allotted for CRF facility (Room No. 111 & 112, ground floor, Chemistry building) houses 22 equipment including various sophisticated instruments such as 500 MHz NMR, Single Crystal XRD, Powder XRD, AFM, SEM, PPMS Dynacool, SQUID, Surface area analyzer, DSC, TGA etc. Rest of the equipment under CRF facility are located at various departments of the institute. The overall day to day activities and the policy of the centre is determined by a committee of faculty of this institute. The CRF committee appointed a faculty in charge for each of the instruments under the centre for smooth running and maintenance the equipment. Further, CRF facility at IIT Jodhpur imparts an opportunity towards its users for accessing various latest and advanced instruments persuasively via a transparent online booking portal. Thus CRF facility contributes its users significantly to publish their research findings in internationally peer reviewed journals. IITJ CRF facility welcomes external users from all national institutes and R & D organizations for accessing this facility on a minimal chargeable basis.
The Main objectives of CRF facility:
Advanced scientific instrumentation service to researchers from both academic and industrial organizations.
R & D for advanced scientific instrumentation.
Technology development and innovation in the area of scientific instrumentation.
Encouraging start-ups for scientific instrumentation.

Welcome To The Artificial Intelligence of Things (AIOT) Fab Facility
Artificial Intelligence of Things (AIOT) is a new technology vertical that has leveraged the convergence of Artificial Intelligence (AI), the Internet of Things (IoT), along with mobile communication technologies, and has led to a massive growth in the intelligent devices that are connected together to solve problems collaboratively.
Artificial Intelligence of Things (AIoT) initiative at IIT Jodhpur aims to create facilities for End-to-End Design, Development, Prototyping, and Delivery of AIOT systems for start-ups, MSMEs, and end-user industries, catering to applications of Industry 4.0, agriculture, healthcare, infrastructure, energy, environment, and water management.
Two important facilities are created for the development of AIOT technologies by faculty and Students of IIT Jodhpur, with participation of industry and other academic partners
AIoT Fab Facility
The Fab is equipped with a set of instruments for the development & characterization of sensors of various kinds that are the foundation of an IoT ecosystem, providing devices with the ability to collect data. The Fab Facility is undergoing further updates with the addition of new instruments. The AIoT Fab also focuses on the development of new sensing materials and targets applications based on photonics, biotechnology, and solar cells. It also envisions developing flexible sensors that can be used for making wearable devices, along with other inertial sensors, utilising MEMS technology.
AIoT Electronic Manufacturing Unit (EMU)
The AIoT EMU Facility is to be equipped with EDA CAD software for designing targeted sensors, signal conditioning & interface circuit, fabless VLSI/FPGA design, & PCB design. The EMU facility will also house Chip assembly/packaging, Electronic component & assembly, along with hardware and software platforms that are required for developing IoT-enabled AI applications. The EMU will also facilitate the commercialization of products with facilities of prototyping, limited manufacturing, and pilot production, in line with stakeholders’ strategy/ direction. The AIoT initiative at IIT Jodhpur is also supported by the AIOT - Innovation Hub Foundation (IHF), which is working as a joint venture Section-8 company between RISL (RajCOMP Info Services Ltd), JCKIF (Jodhpur City Knowledge and Innovation Foundation), and IIT Jodhpur.
